# Bernard Huang - LinkedIn Post

'Information Gain' is the most important concept in SEO right now.

If you use ChatGPT to write a blog post about 'Best CRM Software,' it simply predicts the next most logical word based on the existing internet. By definition, it creates the absolute *average* of what is already out there.

Google's algorithms are looking for Information Gain—new data points, unique perspectives, or expert quotes that do not exist anywhere else in their index. If your B2B content doesn't have it, it won't rank in 2024.

Use AI to scale your processes, but use humans to scale your insights.
