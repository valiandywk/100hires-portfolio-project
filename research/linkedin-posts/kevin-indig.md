# Kevin Indig - LinkedIn Post

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is no longer a future concept; it's happening right now with AI Overviews. Most B2B SaaS companies are still trying to optimize for traditional 10 blue links, completely ignoring that LLMs summarize answers directly in the SERP. If your content doesn't provide a unique, strong opinion or proprietary data, you will be abstracted away by Google's AI.

**My framework for adapting:**
- Stop writing definitive 'What is X' guides. The AI answers that.
- Shift to 'How we solved X using Y data'.
- Optimize for brand mentions and entity associations, not just raw search volume.

The funnel is shifting. Adapt or lose your top-of-funnel traffic.
