# Mike King - LinkedIn Post

The recent Google API leak confirmed what many of us in the technical SEO space have suspected for years regarding how they process semantic relevance.

When you look at features like `siteFocusScore` and how Google uses bi-directional transformers (BERT/MUM), it's clear that keyword stuffing is dead. But what's fascinating is how they weight entities.

If you are using LLMs to generate content, you must ensure the model understands the Knowledge Graph relationships of your topic. Don't just ask ChatGPT to 'write an article.' Give it a list of required entities and semantic triples to weave into the document.
