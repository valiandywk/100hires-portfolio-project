# Ross Simmonds - LinkedIn Post

Create Once. Distribute Forever.

Everyone is talking about using AI to write *more* blog posts. That's the wrong approach. Most B2B SaaS companies already have too many mediocre blog posts.

Instead, use AI to scale your DISTRIBUTION.
- Take your best-performing webinar.
- Feed the transcript into Claude.
- Prompt it to generate 5 LinkedIn posts, 3 Twitter threads, and 1 email newsletter.

AI shouldn't just be an SEO content generator; it should be your content repurposing engine.
