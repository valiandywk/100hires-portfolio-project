Here is a highly tactical ChatGPT prompt I'm using to refresh old B2B SEO content:

'You are an expert SEO copywriter. I am going to give you a blog post that is currently ranking on page 2 for [Keyword]. I want you to read the text, identify any sections where the information is outdated or generic, and suggest 3 highly specific, data-driven additions (Information Gain) that would make this post fundamentally better than the current top-ranking pages.'

Don't use AI to write from scratch. Use it to find the gaps in your existing content.
